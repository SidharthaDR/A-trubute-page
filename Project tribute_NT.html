Beginning:

Born to Kushina Uzumaki a former vessile of Nine tailed Fox and Minato Namikaze who was 4th hokage. Naruto lost his parents as soon as he born, in hands of enraged 9 tailed fox kurama, who's soul was split in half by Minato and sealed half in himself while he was dying and other half into Naruto to decrease its power. For security reasons, Naruto's Last name was kept as his mother's maiden name Uzumaki. Naruto didn't knew about his parents until he grew up.

Childhood:

Naruto, never knew he was vessile of Kurama, the king of tailed beasts. He was bought up under the hidden-supervision of 3rd hokage, who was also mentor of his father's mentor Jiraya-sama.
Due to him being vessil of Nine-Tailed fox, which caused so much destruction, people of village used to be scared and avoid him. Then to gain attention, he used to prank around alot.


Naruto causing mis-chief.

Ramen guy was one of first people, who showed affection to Naruto and treated like a true villager. Naruto's favourate food being Ramen, the kind shop owner of Ichiraku Ramen, used to offer free food once in a while. And it became Naruto's go-to shop since childhood.


Ramen Guy offering food to Naruto.

His class teacher, Iruka Sensei played important role in changing Naruto's perspective of life. When no one believed him and treated him like a human, Iruka was one of first few who could understand Naruto.

Kakashi saved Iruka and Naruto from an Ambush attack.

After few years, Naruto get to join in team 7 alongside his childhood crush Sakura and his frenemy Sasuke Uchiha, under Kakashi sensei.
