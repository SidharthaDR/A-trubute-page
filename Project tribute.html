Naruto Uzumaki

Beginning:

Born to Kushina Namikaze and Minato Namikaze aka 4th hokage. For security reasons, his Last name was kept as his mother's maiden name Uzumaki. Naruto lost his parents as soon as he born, in hands of enraged 9 tailed fox kurama, which was split in half by Minato and sealed in his kid version and Minato himself as he was losing his breath. Naruto didn't knew about his parents until he grew up.

Childhood:

Naruto, never knew he was vessile of Kurama, the king of tailed beasts. He was bought up under the hidden-supervision of 3rd hokage, who was also mentor of his father's mentor Jiraya-sama. Thus, Naruto grew up alone. He was mostly ignored and frowned upon by villagers because he is vessile of Nine tailed fox. Thus, for attention, Naruto used to prank alot, and get into trouble so often.

His first close relation was with a Ramen shop owner, who is a kind hearted man, and gave feeling of home to Naruto with Ramen and love. Followed by Iruka Sensei, who played crucial role in changing Naruto's idea of life.

Iruka sensei holding fainted Naruto, after Kakashi rescued them from a threat

Teenage hero:

Naruto under the supervision of Jiraya sensei, trained hard enough to reach his previous mentors' levels. Jiraya who was also a mentor of Naruto's father, and the one who suggested name "Naruto" when Minato was soon to become a father, was a grand-father figure to Naruto.
Note: Naruto didn't knew about his family yet.



Jiraya Sensei teaching Rasengen, which was actually developed by Naruto's father

Naruto after learning sage mode, neutralized Nagato Uzumaki aka Pain, who was the leader of Akatsuki and also a former desciple of Jiraya Sensei. Thus, saving village and becoming a hero.
